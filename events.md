# Past Events

2020: *Sarao no Pátio das Flores*, a recitation of Narlan's poetry by Luiz Caldas, released. Listen on [Luiz Caldas's webpage](https://www.luizcaldas.com.br/)!

2017: Narlan organized and led the [first delegation of US Poets to Cuba](https://gwhatchet.com/2017/09/07/professor-leads-poet-group-on-historic-trip-to-cuba/).

2017: Narlan's book [*Antología poética bilingüe*](https://narlanmatos.github.io/translations/antologia/) was published in Spain.

2016: Croatian National Radio presented a program about Narlan's life and poetry.

2014: Legendary Italian Poetry journal, *Poesia*, dedicates thirteen pages to Narlan's life and poetry. 

2013: Narlan Matos was an Invited Participant at the [Södermalms Poesifestival](https://sodermalmspoesifestival2013.tumblr.com/) in Stockholm, Sweden

2012: Narlan Matos represented Brazil at the [Bilingual reading](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I_ol-ZwWP0s) at [Druskininkai International Fall Poetic Festival](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TYAV9_idXTc)

2011: Narlan Matos participates in “Com a Palavra o Escritor” (Word with the Writer) Program at the Jorge Amado House Foundation. 

2011: Narlan Matos becomes a member of the Jequié Academy of Letters. [Story in Portuguese](https://miscelaneajuliolucas.blogspot.com/2011/06/narlan-matos-toma-posse-na-academia-de.html)
