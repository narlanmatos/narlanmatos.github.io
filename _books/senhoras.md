  *Senhoras e Senhores: o Amanhecer* is a poetry collection first published in 1997 by Fundação Casa de Jorge Amado.
  Written in Portuguese, it forms part of Narlan Matos’s core poetic corpus.
  
  In 1997, it won the *Prêmio Braskem* da Fundação Casa de Jorge Amado (House of Jorge Amado Foundation).
