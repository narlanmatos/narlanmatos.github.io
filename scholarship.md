# Scholarship

*Alongside his work as a poet and musician, Narlan Matos Teixeira has pursued scholarship in literature, language, and culture.*
<!--His academic work reflects many of the concerns that also shape his poetry—including ...and intercultural dialogue.-->
*Although his primary focus in recent years has been creative writing and international literary exchange, his scholarly background has informed his engagement with literature as both an artistic and critical practice.*
