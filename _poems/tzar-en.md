---

poem_id: tzar-en

title: "Czar"
  
---

<div class="poem"> 
<br>

vast the waiting for everything<br>
for the sea the west conceals and reveals<br>
for the gentle arms of the pale shore<br>
of the sea foam<br>
for the fragrance of lavender<br>
for the meadows and violets<br>
for the lady of the dream with hands of lilies<br>
and jasmine arms<br>
perfumed by the dark night’s chill<br>
<br><br>

immortal is the czar of time<br>
like a samurai hidden in the invisible<br>
flying over our brittle corpses<br>
vocabularies pour from his mouth<br>
in the shape of mountains and streams<br>
in our spirits<br>
the ache of is and exists throbs<br>
in our spirits<br>
nothing brings silence or sleep<br>
and finally<br>
a strange nothing beckons behind things<br>
<br><br>

meanwhile take in the dawns<br>
and the wind<br>
and the gold summer sows across the fields<br>
and the words of March announcing green leaves<br>
take in the dark water of the forest’s rivers<br>
flowing over white sand<br>
take in, brother, what there is of the eternal<br>
<br><br>

because waiting for mankind is vast

</div>
