---

title: "Poetinis Druskinink ruduo 2012 (Drukininkai Poetic fall 2012)"

publication_id: poetinis

overview_description: |

   *Poetinis Druskinink ruduo 2012 (Drukininkai Poetic fall 2012)* is a multilingual edition 
   featuring the poets who participated in the 2012 Druskininkai International Fall Poetic Festival,
   incluing Narlan Matos.
   It contains selected poems in the original language (Portuguese, in Narlan Matos's case) 
   and translations in Lithuanian.
   It contains selected poems from Narlan Matos's book *Elegia ao Novo Mundo*.
