   *Autores Baianos: um Panorama* is a multilingual edition containing Portuguese originals 
   and translations in German, English, and Spanish. It features poetry of Narlan Matos
   and eleven other poets from Bahia, Brazil.
   
   This volume was produced by the state government (Organização Fundação Cultural do Estado da Bahia; FUNCEB)
   for cultural diplomacy and international literary festivals.
